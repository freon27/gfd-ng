German for Developers

I'm a native english-speaker. Even though it's pretty easy to find jobs in Germany that only require English, I like to work in German as much as possible. I studied up to C1 in a languge school before I got my first job. At the end, I came out with fairly decent German but it didn't prepare me for the challenge of talking about software development.

When trying to use German to work as a programmer, understanding when to use the English word and when to use the German equivalent is a minefield. If you decide to use the English version, the next challenge will be how to make it sound German (eindeutschen).

Nouns have to be given a gender and a plural form. Verbs have to be conjugated.
